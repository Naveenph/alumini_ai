About AlumniNet System

The Alumni Management Portal is a centralized networking and collaboration application. Designed to bridge the gap between educational institutions and active professional alumni, our system serves as a digital directory and career support utility for students, alumni, and administrators.

Our Mission

To create a vibrant, active professional ecosystem where students obtain direct access to industry leaders, explore career fields, find mentors, and transition from academics to industry careers.

Safe & Verified

Every alumni and student registration is thoroughly reviewed by the system administrators. Password hashing and authentication mechanisms ensure complete data safety.

Role & Permission Structure

User Role Access & Privileges
System Administrator Full moderation control. Review and approve/reject all student and alumni signups, delete accounts, view logs, create official college events, broadcast system announcements.
Verified Alumni Create and edit professional profiles (linked to LinkedIn), host interactive technical webinars for students, post job listings/referral openings, and message connected mentees.
Registered Student Browse verified alumni directories with search filters, request mentorship connections, register for admin events and webinars, apply for jobs, and receive matching recommendations.
